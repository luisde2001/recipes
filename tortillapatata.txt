Ingredients:
 - 4 eggs
 - 4 potatos
 - 1 onion
 - some salt
 - olive oil

Directions:
 1. Cut the potato on small pieces and fry them as if french frites where, just until they are soft

 2. Fry with them the onion previously cutted in small pieces

 3. include in the pan the mixed eggs, and live it cooking a pair of minutes

 4. give it a roll and turn it upside down in the pan. Another pair of minutes
